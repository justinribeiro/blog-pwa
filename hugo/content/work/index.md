I am a big fan of work, it helps me buy food. I enjoy the curiosity, the creating, the exploration.

I'm not averse to the whole 9-5 affair—I am software architect in an ol' public fintech corp these days ohh la la—but building cool software and running organizations with amazing people is what I'm primarily known for. I am always happy to [consult]() which I've done for three decades and counting, which brings me and your organization great joy and rewards.

Or we could hang out and drink coffee and talk about the philosophy of science as I love doing [research and teaching]()—the PhD helps with that as you can imagine.

And I'll tell you what—I've been known to spin a yarn on a variety of topics in front of a lot different audiences big and small. If you're looking for a [speaker]() I might just be your guy.

So what are you waiting for? An invitation? Okay, I see you. Please get started by reading more at one of the links below.
