I have been busy on a lot of different projects, but the one I'm most excited about is the SX-70 Flash Adapter. I have an amazing photographer out in NYC testing it in studio, and as of today I've got a nice snap case modeling and printed that looks pretty trick if I do say so myself.

My amazing designer, [Laura Perrone](https://www.zynithfoundry.com), is currently working on the art and manual for the device, and it'll get a sweet logo to go along with the case once we iterate a few art concepts.
